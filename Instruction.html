Welcome to the Math Game

Each user will have a set of randomly chosen 10 questions with an absolute different variation of different Equations. You will be able to choose only one answer out of the 4 choices given, after each correct answer you will gain a point, on the other hand, if you chooses the wrong answer, you lose a point. The game ends after a certin amount of time.

The Rules are simple

  • Choose only one answer.
  • 4 minutes on the clock.
  • And choose wisely

Enjoy:)
